About Symail

Symail is a free, open-source email client built for people who value privacy and control over their communication. Designed with end-to-end encryption at its core, Symail keeps your conversations safe from prying eyes while offering a clean, modern interface that makes email simple again.

Unlike mainstream providers, Symail doesn’t compromise your data for profit. Your messages are yours alone. Whether you’re sending personal notes or managing professional communication, Symail combines security with usability so you never have to trade convenience for privacy.

Backed by a transparent, community-driven development process, Symail is constantly improving with the help of open-source contributors. It is more than an email client; it is a statement that your digital privacy matters.
